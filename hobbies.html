About myself

Music

In my spare time, I greatly enjoy playing my violin. I've been playing it since elementary school, and plan to keep going with it. I find that playing violin helps tremendously whenever I've had a bad day. Music helps to put my mind at ease whenever I'm feeling stressed.

My Dad also plays violin and listening to his playing growing up was one of the main reasons I wanted to learn. Whenever I visit back home we play duets together. My personal favourite is the J.S Bach double violin concerto in D Minor.

Esports

I also enjoy competing with friends in the Esports arena for the game series Splatoon. Splatoon is a 3rd person shooter developed by Nintendo, with the added mechanic that the players shoot paint at each other, and can use this paint to sneak through terrain or climb walls. While seemingly simple upfront, this game has a surprising amount of depth to it. I've been playing Splatoon since the first game, and have been dedicated to pushing my favorite weapon:
Carbon Roller Deco
I've played for two teams so far. One with my older brother, and one with my university. I'm proud to say I've accomplished quite a bit with each team.
